about-me

Hi, I'm Hannah


I’m a fourth-year Graphic Design major at SVSU, hailing from Clarkston, MI. Living on campus has provided me with a vibrant creative environment where I’ve worked on various design projects for the university. My passion for visual storytelling drives me to explore different styles and mediums, allowing me to bring fresh ideas to life.


In addition to design, I’m a dancer, which inspires my creativity and adds rhythm to my work. I’m also pursuing a minor in coding, which enables me to blend technology with design and create interactive experiences. I’m excited to continue developing my skills and contributing to the artistic community around me.
